# YEB Product Principles v1.0

## Permanent Product Rules
1. YEB is a decision-support platform, not a tip service.
2. YEB does not promise returns.
3. YEB explains why a score or portfolio was produced.
4. Dashboard shows summary.
5. YEB PRO shows detail and management.
6. AI outputs must be understandable by a non-expert.
7. Numerical evidence must be available for advanced users.
8. Portfolio quality is more important than a single-stock score.
9. Diversification must not be cosmetic.
10. Low correlation cannot justify weak stocks.
11. Existing user workflows must not be broken by new features.
12. New features are additive.
13. Portfolios must be horizon-specific.
14. Portfolio reports must include both numeric and narrative explanation.
15. The user makes the final investment decision.

## Language Rules
Avoid:
- “Kesin alın”
- “Kesin yükselir”
- “Garantili”
- “Kaçırmayın”

Prefer:
- “Belirlenen kriterleri güçlü karşılıyor”
- “Portföy güven endeksi”
- “Tahmini”
- “Karar destek”
- “Mevcut veriler ışığında”

## UI Rule
First view:
- concise
- ranked
- comparable
- mobile-friendly

Expanded view:
- methodology
- component scores
- correlation details
- sector reasoning
- risk reasoning
